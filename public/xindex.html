technology

Modulhaus represents a network of indpendent, positively motivated, radical, collaborative, open source hackers. They posses an immense agency in their skill and understanding of myriad technologies. The agents of modulhaus are representatives of this type. Modulhaus exists to enable and promote the work of our network, by various means, according to individual, or group, interests.

We offer an innovative service for contracting web development. The innovations are based on a rapidly growing complex of modular, open source code, and a distributed laborforce that can design and write it. Read more about web development.

Modulhaus also represents many open source projects, and the potential to quickly build applications and services with them. We are actively building out some of these, experimenting with others. How will they develop? Read more about projects.

Modulhaus represents many creative hackers. Our interest in web development extends to the multi-media production, games, and interactive learning. Our interest in interfaces extpands into the 3rd and 4th dimensions. Our interest in hardware is producing promising prototypes.
